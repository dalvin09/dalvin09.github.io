## Rationale

AUDUSD was spiking lower into the close of the current profile with the dollar pulling. The read was that the spike could develop into a tail, and with the dollar adding upward pressure, if the next profile respected the spike and printed letters back inside the prior profile, odds were good for a run to the other end of range.

---

## Execution

The next profile initially cooperated, printing back inside and confirming the tail thesis. However, once the dollar found legs within its balance range, the upward pressure stalled and the trade couldn't hold. Price reversed and stopped out the position at 0.71506. The market did eventually move higher — the read was right, the position sizing and stop placement didn't survive the noise.

---

## Mistake / Lesson

Position sizing on AUDUSD needs adjustment. With 10 lots, the stop had no room to breathe through the dollar's balance range chop. A smaller size would have allowed the trade to stay alive long enough to capture the move that ultimately played out.
