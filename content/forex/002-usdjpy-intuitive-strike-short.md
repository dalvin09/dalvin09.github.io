## Rationale

Around 2am the dollar began moving, but the overall context was still a balance range so the initial read was noise. As the London session developed, the yen pushed into a relative high based on previous profile structure, which triggered the Intuitive Strike entry short. The thesis was simple: no acceptance above, price goes lower.

---

## Execution

The trade worked initially, confirming the fade thesis. However, when the current period failed to take out the profile's halfback on the move lower, that was the signal the trade wasn't going to play out as expected. The dollar found legs and reversed, stopping the position out at 159.252.

---

## Mistake / Lesson

Balance ranges in the dollar are bad for business. A directional fade setup requires the market to be in a trending or at least a breakout context — inside a balance range the dollar can find support at any level, making stop-outs far more likely even when the initial read is correct.
