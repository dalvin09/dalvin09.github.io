## Rationale

Market had been ripping recently but the morning tempo on the upside was weakening.
We broke the overnight high with **no acceptance** — price couldn't hold above it.

At this point value had yet to build, overlapping all week with no clear directional commitment.

**Thesis:** Failed breakout above the overnight high → market likely builds overlapping value → closes the gap below → grinds lower all day.

---

## Execution

The move played out as expected — sold off after the overnight high rejection.

However, we **failed to one-time frame down through yesterday's value**, which was the key tell.
Once that failed, the odds immediately shifted back in favor of breakaway value to the upside.

Closed the put at $1.75 when the thesis was invalidated.

---

## Mistake / Lesson

Should have closed the put **and immediately flipped long** — doubled the position in the other direction once it was clear the downside one-time framing failed.

The read was correct on both sides. The execution was only half right.
