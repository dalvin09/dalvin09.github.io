Setup: VAL Rejection Long (Forex)

April 17, 2026 · 1 min · Dalvin
Table of Contents

  • Entry
  • Historical Examples

Concept#

Based on Auction Market Theory (AMT). If the market spends time below the Value Area Low overnight and then rotates back inside value at a session open, it signals a failed auction — buyers are in control and price typically seeks the Point of Control (POC) or Value Area High (VAH).

Criteria (All must be met)#

  • Price trades below prior day VAL during overnight/off-hours
  • Price rotates back above VAL on a 5m close at London or NY open
  • No major news in the next 30 minutes
  • Volume confirms (above average on entry candle)
  • HTF (1H/4H) trend is neutral to bullish

Entry#

  • Trigger: First 5m candle to close back above VAL
  • Entry: Close of trigger candle
  • Stop: Below the overnight swing low
  • Target 1 (50%): POC
  • Target 2 (50%): VAH or prior day high

Historical Examples#

  • 001 — GBPUSD Long | VAL Rejection

Notes#

Works best on GBP/USD, EUR/USD, and GBP/JPY during London open. Avoid on high-impact news days (CPI, NFP, FOMC).
